Chapter1

-A mighty lion was trapped in a hunter's net.
-He roared loudly, but a tiny mouse appeared.
-The lion laughed, thinking the mouse could not help.
-The mouse chewed the ropes, freeing the lion.
-Never underestimate anyone; small, kind actions make a difference.
